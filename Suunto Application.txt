Describe the tools/app you are working with. What are the key reasons people use your offering? What are the key features?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This is a professional desktop health and workout companion app built using .NET and Avalonia UI. It is currently in a public beta/open-source prototyping phase, with plans to transition into a premium commercial desktop utility (utilizing a local-first, privacy-respecting one-time license or subscription model) for the broader athletic and fitness communities.

The application allows Suunto watch owners to ingest their offline training files, combine them with nutritional history (via Cronometer), and securely sync their Suunto smartwatch telemetry (HRV, sleep quality, recovery, active calories) using OAuth2. It provides hybrid athletes with advanced training readiness scores, Estimated 1RM tracking, and local-first data privacy.

Key Features & Reasons for Use:
- Absolute Data Privacy & Local Ownership: The application is designed to be 100% offline-first. Personal health metrics, biometrics, and GPS workout coordinates are never uploaded to a third-party cloud.
- Multi-Source Telemetry Stitching: The core algorithm automatically cross-references and stitches strength-training sessions (Hevy) with cardiovascular effort, sleep profiles, and recovery telemetry fetched from Suunto devices within matching ±15-minute time windows.
- Adaptive Metabolic Analytics (TDEE Engine): Computes accurate dynamic Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) and target caloric requirements using smoothed exponential moving averages (EMA) of weight changes mapped against daily nutritional ingestion.
- Recovery, Strain, & Readiness Matrix: Combines Suunto sleep duration and resting HRV (relative to a 60-day running average) with training volume to provide users with a daily training readiness score.

What features would you like to offer as part of Suunto watches? Describe in high level on what type of features you would like to provide to Suunto watches (The possible future suunto watch app for logging workouts):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
I am interested in designing a wrist-based strength and conditioning logging app ("Gym Log Companion") that runs directly on Suunto watches as a SuuntoPlus Sport App. The goal of this watch app is to allow users to log their resistance training sessions (exercises, sets, repetitions, and weights) directly from their wrist, eliminating the need to carry a mobile phone on the gym floor.

Key high-level features:
1. Wrist-Based Set & Rep Logging: An intuitive user interface to quickly log completed sets, select weights, and record repetitions during a workout.
2. Auto-Rest Timer: An automatic countdown timer triggered immediately upon logging a completed set, with haptic and audio notifications when the next set should begin.
3. Real-Time Volume & Load Tracking: On-watch calculations displaying cumulative training volume (kg/lbs) and number of completed hard sets per muscle group for the current session.
4. Relative Intensity & Heart Rate Integration: Real-time tracking of heart rate recovery intervals between sets to estimate cardiovascular strain during anaerobic training.
5. Seamless API Sync: Direct synchronization of structured set-by-set logs back to the Suunto Cloud, allowing external health hubs to stitch strength volume with daily active recovery telemetry.

3. TARGET AUDIENCE & WHO IS IT FOR?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Field: Who is this application for? / Target Audience]
The target audience consists of dedicated, high-intent Suunto watch owners who practice "hybrid training"—athletes who engage in both heavy resistance training (powerlifting, bodybuilding, CrossFit) and multi-sport endurance athletics. 

Currently, strength athletes often feel their gym logs (tracked in popular platforms like Hevy) are siloed away from their cardiovascular and sleep recovery data tracked on their Suunto watches. This professional-grade desktop application is built specifically for this active sub-segment of Suunto users, providing them with a premium, local dashboard to bridge the gap between strength and endurance metrics. It is designed to launch as a commercial desktop application to support ongoing professional-grade development.

4. INNOVATION MINDSET & BRAND FIT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Field: Describe on a high level the innovation mindset behind this Solution]
- Innovative Multi-Source Ingestion: The application automatically correlates gym volume and hard sets logged in strength trackers with Suunto's recovery and HRV telemetry within precise time windows. This provides a holistic training strain and daily readiness matrix that does not exist in standard platforms.
- Promotes Brand Loyalty: By making Suunto watches a central hub for strength and hybrid athlete metrics, it encourages gym enthusiasts and hybrid lifters to choose Suunto as their primary wearable ecosystem.
- 100% Data Portability & Privacy: Fits modern consumer demand for data ownership. Users run the app locally, holding all personal biometrics inside a local SQLite database with enterprise-grade native encryption, giving Suunto customers a secure, private dashboard option.
- Premium Business Model Alignment: Our transition to a commercial paid product in the future targets dedicated, high-value athletes who invest in premium gear, mirroring Suunto's premium brand positioning (e.g., Suunto Race or Vertical).
